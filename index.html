Meet Me

Behind the Camera

Norman started out as a painter who fell in love with creating stories using visual depictions. He is based in California. Norman sees photography as a natural extension for his passion of arts. Ready to take on the world, Norman explores nature in its element. It's no surprise that he was voted Top 10 photographer at the International Arts Conference. Norman has a wife with two children whom he enjoys spending time with.
